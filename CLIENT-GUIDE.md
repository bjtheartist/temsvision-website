# TemsVision Website - Content Management Guide

Welcome! This guide shows you how to update photos and content on your website.

---

## Quick Start

**Your Content Studio:** https://temsvision.sanity.studio/

Log in with your Sanity account to manage all your website content.

---

## Managing Gallery Photos

### How to Add/Edit a Photo Category

1. Go to https://temsvision.sanity.studio/
2. Click **"Gallery"** in the left sidebar
3. Click **"Photo Categories"**
4. Click on the category you want to edit (e.g., "Portraits")

### Adding New Photos to a Category

1. Open the category you want to update
2. Scroll to the **"Photos"** section
3. Click **"Add item"**
4. Click **"Upload"** and select your photos
5. **Drag to reorder** - photos at the top appear first
6. Click **"Publish"** (green button, top right) to make changes live

### Creating a New Category

1. Go to **Gallery â†’ Photo Categories**
2. Click **"Create new"** (or the + icon)
3. Fill in:
   - **Category Name** (e.g., "Headshots")
   - **URL Slug** - click "Generate" to auto-create
   - **Short Description** - shown on the gallery card
   - **Cover Image** - the main image for this category
   - **Photos** - all photos in this category
   - **Display Order** - lower numbers appear first (1, 2, 3...)
4. Click **"Publish"**

## Updating Hero Slideshow

The hero section is the big slideshow at the top of your website.

1. Go to https://temsvision.sanity.studio/
2. Click **"Gallery"** in the left sidebar
3. Click **"Hero Slideshow"**
4. Add/remove/reorder images in the **"Slideshow Images"** section
5. Adjust **"Slide Duration"** if you want images to change faster/slower
6. Click **"Publish"**

**Tip:** Use your best 3-5 photos that represent your style!

## Photo Tips

### Recommended Photo Sizes
- **Hero Slideshow:** 2400px wide (landscape orientation works best)
- **Gallery Photos:** 1800-2400px wide
- **Cover Images:** 1200-1800px wide

### Formats
- **JPEG** - best for photos (smaller file size)
- **PNG** - if you need transparency
- **WebP** - excellent quality + small size (recommended)

### File Size
- Keep photos under **2MB** each for fast loading
- Sanity will automatically optimize your uploads

## Common Tasks

### Reorder Photos in a Category
1. Open the category
2. In the Photos section, **drag and drop** to reorder
3. Photos at the top appear first when visitors browse
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Delete a Photo
1. Open the category
2. Hover over the photo you want to remove
3. Click the **trash icon**
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Change Category Cover Image
1. Open the category
2. Click on the **Cover Image**
3. Click **"Clear"** to remove, then **"Upload"** a new one
4. Click **"Publish"**
